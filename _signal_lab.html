Signal Regime Descriptions
Coiled for Breakout (Unicorn Bullish)
Physics Energy depleted (≤ 15), Temporal Energy surging (> 50), divergence extreme (> 80). The narrative spring is loaded with new fuel. 84.6% win rate, +4.1% avg 7-day return across 39 backtested instances. The rarest bullish signal.
Blow-Off Top (Unicorn Bearish)
Both energy lines maxed (≥ 80) AND Physics Energy suddenly collapsing (velocity ≤ -5). Only 14 occurrences in the entire dataset. Everything was at peak and then broke simultaneously.
Peak Narrative (Sharpe 1.82)
Narrative Energy and Physics Energy both above 80. Peak narrative saturation. 61.5% decline rate over 7 days. The strongest backtested short signal (1.82 annualized Sharpe on 963 point-in-time observations).
Losing Steam
Physics Energy dropping > 5 points in 3 days while Narrative Energy holds high. Real energy draining while the story persists. Signals an extreme move; direction determined by Temporal Energy.
Fading Story
Narrative Energy high (≥ 80) but Physics Energy depleted (≤ 20). The story persists but has no real energy underneath. 53.5% decline probability. Often precedes a slow bleed.
Danger Zone
Narrative Energy high, Physics Energy low (≤ 30), and Narrative Pressure elevated (≥ 15). All three danger signals converging. NRS, overreaction, and coordination all firing together.
Timing Catalyst (Direction Key)
The critical differentiator. Low energy_t (< 15) = crash setup with -9.06% avg return. High energy_t (> 50) = rally setup with +9.17% avg return. Separates crashes from rallies when other signals look identical.
Building Momentum
Narrative Energy low (≤ 30) while Physics Energy high (≥ 60). New energy entering while old narrative decays. Bullish re-acceleration with 55.8% gain rate. The only regime with a positive median return.
