Frequently asked

Common questions about narrative intelligence.

What is Market Prism?
Market Prism is a forensic narrative intelligence platform for equity markets — built for traders who got tired of being the exit liquidity on stories that didn't match the filings. Submit any stock thesis (a sentence, a headline, or an investment memo) and Market Prism pattern-matches it against 3.5 million historical observations: every earnings call, 10-K, 10-Q, 8-K, news headline, and social post we've embedded across 150+ tickers. In under 250 milliseconds it returns a deterministic narrative hit-rate, predicted 5/10/20-day returns, and the closest historical analogues — anchored to the SEC filings the narrative is allegedly tracking, weighted by source authority, and flagged when the same framing has been coordinated across multiple outlets in a tight time window. Same input always produces the same output, so you can show your work — and audit ours.
How does narrative intelligence work?
Every earnings call, SEC filing, news headline, and social post about a tracked ticker is converted into a 384-dimensional embedding plus seven forensic scores (Narrative Force, Risk Pressure, Coordination, Decay, Drift, Reversal, and Mass). When you submit a new thesis, Market Prism finds its 200 nearest neighbors in vector space and aggregates what actually happened next.
What is a narrative hit-rate?
The narrative hit-rate is the percentage of historical analogues to your thesis that resolved in the predicted direction within a given holding period. A 72% hit-rate over 10 days means 72% of the 200 closest historical narratives moved with the thesis over the following 10 trading days.
Is Market Prism free to use?
Yes — narrative validation is free on the homepage with no login required. The full platform (dashboard, ticker pages, daily brief, sector heatmap, methodology, paper portfolio) is $25/month billed annually.
What stocks does Market Prism cover?
Market Prism actively tracks 150+ US equities including NVDA, TSLA, AAPL, MSFT, META, AMZN, GOOGL, AMD, PLTR, and most large-cap names with meaningful narrative volume. Coverage updates every trading day before US market open.
Is Market Prism financial advice?
No. Market Prism is a research and analytics platform for informational purposes only. All verdicts, scores, and signals are deterministic analytical outputs — not investment recommendations. Users should conduct independent research and consult a licensed financial advisor before making investment decisions.
