How it works

A deterministic AI for equity narratives.

Three layers, one daily verdict per ticker. No black boxes, no model drift — every signal is reproducible from the same inputs.

01
Ingest
We pull every earnings call, filing, news headline, and post about a tracked ticker the moment it lands. Each becomes one observation — a "narrative dot."
Coverage · live
02
Embed & score
Each dot gets a 384-dimensional embedding plus seven forensic scores — Narrative Force, Risk Pressure, Coordination, Decay, and three more. Stored in pgvector.
L2-normalized · cosine
03
Pattern-match
Drop in any new claim. We find its 200 nearest historical neighbors, aggregate their actual returns, and return a hit-rate, confidence band, and the analogues themselves.
Reproducible · auditable
