The three forces

One chart. Three signals.
Every story, measured.

Price alone tells you where the market went. Market Prism overlays three live forces on top of it so you can see why — and what's likely next.

Signal 1
▲ Narrative Force
Composite strength of the story today — physics energy plus remaining fuel. Rising means momentum is building; falling means the story is running out of road.
Read: Rising = bullish build-up. Flat at top = exhausted.
Signal 2
▼ Risk Pressure
NRS-weighted risk score. Combines narrative risk, overreaction, and coordination into one gauge. Spikes above 20 flag that forensic risk is piling up — fade territory.
Read: Above 20 = fade the rally. Below 10 = clean path.
Signal 3
● Narrative Mass
How much institutional coverage a story has attracted and how fast it's accelerating. Big dots mean the narrative is loud; green means bullish keywords, red means bearish.
Read: Big dot + red = peak trap. Big dot + green = building.
Every chart inside Signal Lab overlays these three forces on live price — so you can see the story building, peaking, or breaking before price catches up.
